Rules

Before joining our community it is important you have read & understood the rules outlined on this page to avoid being banned.

Rule #1
No advertisements for anything without prior permission from an Admin. With the exception of free to attend Pokémon Go community/group gatherings.
Rule #2
No requests for coordinates or how-to spoof/bot.
Rule #3
No fake posts. Especially in #sightings, #nests and #tasks channels.
Rule #4
No bullying, harassment, trolling, or heated arguments.
Rule #5
No adult (18+), NSFW, explicit, cursing, racist, condescending or controversial content.
Rule #6
No offensive or inappropriate names.
Rule #7
No posting articles from pokemongohub.
Rule #8
No chatting in non-chat channels (i.e. channels without ‘chat’ in its channel name).
Rule #9
When posting to a non-chat channel, read pinned posts for recommended post formats and channel-specific rules before posting.
Rule #10
Do use common sense.

That's it. If you're ever in doubt, just use common sense.
